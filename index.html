About Me

Ginny is a 20-something web developer who resides on the north side of Indianapolis. She has always been interested in coding but went down other paths first. Currently, she is learning JavaScript at Eleven Fifty Academy. She also has a BS in Media Arts & Science from IUPUI and a nanodegree from Udacity in Digital Marketing.
Outside of coding, Ginny enjoys video editing, photography, and power soccer. One of her short films has won second place in a film contest. Her power soccer team is consistently in the top league and has finished in the top three at nationals 7 out of her 13 seasons.
